# Lead Research Agent Prompt Library

Use these prompts to guide your AI agent:

1) Qualification criteria
- "Score this lead from 0-100 based on ICP: [describe ICP]. Justify the score in 1 sentence."

2) Enrichment
- "Find the decision maker's name, job title, and verified email for domain: {{domain}}."

3) Personalization
- "Write a 2-sentence opener referencing a recent post or initiative by {{company}} relevant to {{problem}}."
